# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone.
Participate with empathy, evaluate technical claims on their merits, give actionable
feedback, respect privacy, and avoid discriminatory, threatening, sexualized, or
disruptive behavior.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior
harms the community. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository
maintainers. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, with attention to
the safety of the reporter and affected participants.
